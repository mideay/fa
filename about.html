About us

We are an organization that connects Africans living in Canada and allies to improve qualities of life, create platforms for sustainable social and economic developments through supporting new immigrants and connecting them to resources that enables them to contribute to the Canadian economy and also support their families back home.

Our Vision

To build a global Network of Africans and Allies, where young people can find and strengthen their voice in making positive contributions in advancing the Canadian culture, economy while also projecting Africa's socio economic development. This would help build a healthy community where everyone belongs and thrives.

Our Mission

To Improve quality of life and socio-economic conditions of African's; one individual, one community at a time. In addition to making meaningful contributions to the Canadian economy. We aim to add more values to people's lives everyday by valuing integrity and equal opportunities.
